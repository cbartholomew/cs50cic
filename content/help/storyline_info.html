The Story

The premise of the story is that some US government agency doesn’t have the funding or resources (what’s new?) to place a real spy full-time on campus to investigate suspicious events that involve corruption, unwanted propaganda, and events, which could end the world (pretty scary stuff).

You’re approached by your purposed agent handler, Clark Johnson, who wishes recruit you. Clark knows that you are familiar with both hardware and software fundamentals (Always remember that a computer enthusiast, such as yourself, is a great asset to this world).

Will you help Clark Johnson stop the chaos that could potentially lead to the end of the world?
